B&B · Monopoli

More than a stay.
An experience.

Villa Maria Pia was born from a passion for genuine hospitality. A bright villa just steps from the heart of Monopoli, where every detail is crafted to make you feel truly at home — not as a passing guest, but as someone who was warmly expected.

The rooms are spacious, immaculate and bathed in natural light. The shared spaces invite you to unwind. And the atmosphere — that of a home lived with care and love — is felt from the very first moment you step through the door.

The location, gently set back from the centre, offers the rare quiet of a real holiday — without sacrificing the ease of reaching the sea, the old town and the finest corners of authentic Puglia in just a few minutes.

Where we are

Monopoli and Puglia
within easy reach

Villa Maria Pia is located in a quiet area just a few minutes from the centre of Monopoli and its beautiful beaches.

The historic centre, with its white streets, local restaurants and charming harbour, is easy to reach and represents the most authentic side of the city.

The villa is also an excellent base for discovering Puglia, with places such as Polignano a Mare, Alberobello and the Valle d’Itria within easy reach.

A perfect balance between relaxation and exploration.

Monopoli historic centre 5 min on foot
Sea & beaches 10 min on foot
Polignano a Mare 15 min by car
Alberobello 40 min by car
Bari Airport 45 min by car
